About ASARECA

The Association for Strengthening Agricultural Research in Eastern and Central Africa (ASARECA) is a non-profit intergovernmental organization established in 1994 by 11 member countries.

We facilitate and coordinate agricultural research for development in the region to promote economic growth, food security, and sustainable natural resource management.
