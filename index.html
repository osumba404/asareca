About ASARECA

A not-for-profit Inter-Governmental Organization owned by National Agricultural Research Institutes (NARIs) of 15 member States in Eastern and Central Africa

Our Mission

To contribute to increased productivity, commercialization, and competitiveness of the ECA Agricultural Sector through strengthening, catalyzing, and coordinating agricultural research for development in the ECA Sub Region.

Our Vision

A transformed ECA Agricultural Sector supporting improved livelihoods, sustained economic growth, and inclusive development.

Our Core Values

Excellence, Integrity, Innovation, Partnership, and Inclusivity in agricultural research and development.

Our Key Programs

Driving agricultural transformation through research, innovation, and capacity building
